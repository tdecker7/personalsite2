My Story

Hailing from Southern Indiana, and now residing in Boulder, CO, you can find me biking the trails in search of a brew, gardening, brewing a batch of beer, or tucked away somewhere trying to hack together some projects I'm passionate about!

I studied Information Technology and Computer Science at Indiana State University, in my hometown. I graduated in 2015 Cum Laude, and since I've been working in IT. I've held positions from infrastructure and software support, to IT Management.

Along the way, I've garnered valuable experience, which has led me to my decision to pursue a career in Development. Currently, I'm a student at Galvanize - Platte, in Denver, CO.
